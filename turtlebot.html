Vision based Localization and Navigation of Unmanned Vehicles in an indoor area

Reserach Project

  • Developed alternative localization and navigation method for unmanned vehicles in an indoor G.P.S. deprived environment.
  • Integrated visual fiducial markers, April Tags, to provide position data to the Turtle bot (unmanned vehicle), into 3D simulated lab environment developed in Gazebo.
  • Increased obstacle avoidance rate by 300% by incorporating Gmaping (machine learning library) into the multi agent system.
