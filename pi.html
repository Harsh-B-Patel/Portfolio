IOT MotionSpyware

  • Expectation: After a break in at my apartment, my dad became a bit paranoid. Hence this Spyware was made to reinforce security.
  • This Spyware uses a Raspberry Pi and a motion sensor.
  • This Algorithm is coded in C. It uses GPIO pins to connect the Pi to a motion sensor. Upon sensing a movement the algorithm sends a IFTTT request online, which sends a email to the user. This email sends details to the user such as time of motion and location.
